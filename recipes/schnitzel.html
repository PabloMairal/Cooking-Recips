Schnitzel Recipe

Description

This is a super easy recipe of schnitzel (also known as milanesa in Argentina), you will need some chicken breast, which will be marinated with eggs and some condiments and spices, and after that you will cover it with breadcrumbs and finally frying it.

You could also use pork or other meat, it's up to you!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts (2 large breasts)
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 table spoon of paprika
  • 1 table spoon of sesame seeds (optional)
  • Salt as needed
  • Oil for frying (don't use olive oil)
  • Fresh lemon wedges for garnish(optional)
